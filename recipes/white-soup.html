Ingredients

  • 1kg catfish
  • 1kg yam
  • 2 cups of ground crayfish
  • 2 tablespoons of ground pepper
  • 1 cup of uziza seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Optional: Ogiri for flavor

Steps

  1. Peel and cut the yam into small pieces, then boil until soft.
  2. Blend the boiled yam with some water to form a smooth paste.
  3. In a pot, add the catfish and enough water to cover it. Cook until the fish is tender.
  4. Add the yam paste to the pot and stir well to combine.
  5. Add ground crayfish, pepper, uziza seeds, and salt. Stir and let it simmer for about 10 minutes.
  6. If using, add ogiri for additional flavor and cook for another 5 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with pounded yam or any preferred swallow.
